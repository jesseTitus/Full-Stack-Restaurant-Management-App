Our Story

Little Lemon is a classic "old school" family diner located in London, Ontario. We offer quality food for the whole family at reasonable prices. At Little Lemon, we treat our customers like family and make you feel at home. Our diner has been serving the community for over 65 years and continues to be a staple for locals and tourists alike. Our team is dedicated to providing the best dining experience possible, which is why we use only the freshest ingredients. Come and join us for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, and experience the Little Lemon difference.
